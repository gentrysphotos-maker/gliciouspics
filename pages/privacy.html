Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 23, 2026

G.Licious Pics ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and disclose your personal details when you visit our website or purchase fine art prints from us.

1. Information We Collect

When you browse our store or make a purchase, we collect the personal information you provide to us, including:

  • Customer Details: Your full name, email address, phone number, and shipping/billing addresses.
  • Order Information: The specific prints, sizes, and materials you purchase.
  • Device Information: Technical data including your IP address, browser type, and navigation habits, collected automatically when browsing our pages to help improve site performance and security.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to operate our business and serve you, specifically to:

  • Process and fulfill your print orders securely.
  • Communicate with you regarding your order status, including sending confirmation and shipping emails.
  • Respond to your questions, contact forms, or custom order requests.
  • Analyze site usage to optimize our layout, performance, and catalog presentation.

3. Sharing with Third-Party Providers

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We share your information only with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our site and fulfilling your orders:

  • Stripe: Payment processing. When you proceed to checkout, you are redirected to Stripe-hosted checkouts. All billing and card transaction data are processed directly by Stripe on their secure PCI-compliant servers. **We do not store or see your credit card details.**
  • Prodigi: Print fulfillment and shipping. Your name, shipping address, phone number, and print options are shared with our print partner, Prodigi, to produce and deliver your physical prints directly to your door.
  • Resend: Email notifications. We use Resend to deliver transactional emails, including order receipts and shipping confirmation updates.

4. Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. All communications between your browser and our website, as well as connections to Stripe, are protected using Industry-Standard Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) encryption technology.

5. Cookies

We use basic cookies to keep track of items stored in your local shopping cart (`localStorage`). These cookies do not contain any personally identifiable information and are used solely to preserve your checkout state as you browse between gallery categories.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about you, request corrections to any inaccuracies, or request that we delete your details. If you wish to make a request, please contact us using the details below.
