About the Artist

G.Licious Pics

My name is Gentry, aka G.Licious. I have been curious about nature and the earth since I was a kid — always drawn to landscapes, animals, textures, and colors.

I discovered photography as a way to capture these beautiful things and have them as permanent memories. Over 10 years behind the lens, I've had the chance to travel to some extraordinary places.

The vibrant beauty of Hawaiʻi is my main muse. Through bold and colorful subjects, each photograph tells a unique story — reflecting the essence of Hawaiʻi's natural wonders and rich topography, with a few other favorite locations sprinkled in.
